Chicken and Banana's Wobbly Quest

By Rocky Hollen and Jenn Krenik

Images by Cavin Krenik

Editing by the Collective Mind

Published by L2 Publishing

Page 1

In Whizzy-Woo Forest, where colors all zoom,
Lived Chicken, a hen with a bright red plume!
She clucked and she solved with a peck and a grin,
So clever, so sprightly, her heart all a-spin.
But Banana, oh my, with his glasses so round,
Would trip and then tumble right down to the ground!
His peel was so shiny, his heart was so cheery,
He giggled and wiggled, though falling was dreary.

Page 2

One day on a path that was sparkly and bright,
Chicken met Banana—oh, what a sight!
“Why, hello!” clucked Chicken, “Let’s be pals, you and me!”
Banana just beamed, “Yes, oh, yippee-dee-dee!”

Page 3

But then came a cry, so tiny, so shrill,
From Squeaky Squirrel on a polka-dot hill.
“My acorn hat’s gone! It’s shiny, it’s fine!”
Chicken and Banana said, “We’ll make it all mine!”

Page 4

Off they went skipping to the Bouncy-Bop Bridge,
With wobbles and hops like a dance on a ridge.

Page 5

Banana slipped fast with a “Whoops!” and a “Whee!”
He flopped on his peel, crying, “Oh, clumsy me!”

Page 6

Chicken just clucked, “Up you go, don’t you frown!”
And she helped her friend rise with a skip and a bound.

Page 7

Through Whizzy-Woo Forest, they scampered with glee,
To find that lost hat by the Tickle-Tree Sea.

Page 8

Then up rolled Grumpy Grape, all purple and sour,
He grumbled and mumbled for hour after hour.
“That hat’s in the Grove, where the tickle-trees grow,
But those leaves will tickle from your head to your toes!”

Page 9

Chicken looked nervous, her comb all a-quiver,
But Banana just laughed, “We’ll be brave, won’t we, shiver?”

Page 10

To Tickle-Tree Grove they dashed with a zoom,
Where leaves giggled wildly and danced in the gloom.

Page 11

Banana then slipped on a mushroom so slick,
He rolled and he tumbled, oh, what a trick!

Page 12

Chicken just clucked, “Let’s solve this, and quick!”
She dodged tickling leaves with a twirl and a flick.

Page 13

Banana cheered loud, his heart big and bold,
“Keep going, dear Chicken, you’re worth more than gold!”

Page 14

At last, in a tree with a shimmer and shine,
They spotted the hat—oh, it looked so divine!

Page 15

Back to Squeaky they ran, with the hat held up high,
She squealed and she danced ‘neath the Whizzy-Woo sky.

Page 16

The forest all partied ‘til the moon gave a glow,
With giggling flowers and a polka-dot show.

Page 17

Together they learned, as they whirled in a twirl,
That teamwork’s the best in their Whizzy-Woo world!

Page 18

So Chicken and Banana, with a wink and a grin,
Promised more quests where their fun would begin!
