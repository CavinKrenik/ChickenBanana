Page 1

In Whizzy-Woo Forest, where colors all zoom,
Lived Chicken, a hen with a bright red plume!
She clucked and she solved with a peck and a grin,
So clever, so sprightly, her heart all a-spin.
But Banana, oh my, with his glasses so round,
Would trip and then tumble right down to the ground!
His peel was so shiny, his heart was so cheery,
He giggled and wiggled, though falling was dreary.

Page 2

One day on a path that was sparkly and bright,
Chicken met Banana—oh, what a sight!
“Why, hello!” clucked Chicken, “Let’s be pals, you and me!”
Banana just beamed, “Yes, oh, yippee-dee-dee!”

Page 18

So Chicken and Banana, with a wink and a grin,
Promised more quests where their fun would begin!
